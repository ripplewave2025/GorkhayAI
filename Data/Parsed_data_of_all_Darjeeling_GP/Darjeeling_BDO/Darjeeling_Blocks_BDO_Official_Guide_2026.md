## General Rule: Who Should People Write To?

| Type of Work / Issue                          | Who to Write To                                      | Why |
|-----------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|-----|
| **Village-level certificates** (Income, Caste, Residential, etc.) | **The Pradhan**, [GP Name] Gram Panchayat           | GP handles most local certificates |
| **Land mutation, basic services, local complaints** | **The Pradhan** of concerned Gram Panchayat         | First point of contact |
| **MGNREGA, PMAY, pensions, block schemes**    | **The Block Development Officer (BDO)**             | BDO is in charge of block-level implementation |
| **Escalated complaints, big issues**          | BDO + copy to **District Magistrate, Darjeeling**   | For higher attention |
| **Police / Law & Order related**              | Superintendent of Police or local Police Station    | Separate from BDO/GP |

**Tip for Gorkhay AI:**  
When user says “letter to BDO” or mentions a block, auto-fill the BDO’s name + email + standard address.  
When user says a specific GP name (from Sthalagala Pattice), auto-fill “The Pradhan, [GP Name] Gram Panchayat”.
